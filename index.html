About Faith Aquatic

Hello Everyone!

I am Desmond and I have been in this shrimp hobby since 2018. I started with a planted tank initially with a few Fire Red Neocaridina shrimps but had little success keeping them. Then, I slowly began my search for answers on several online forums & websites but I still couldn't find any reliable source of information.

Along the way, I met some nice local breeders & reached out to them for help. Slowly, my interest and passion for shrimps grew even more. Hence, I want to give back by sharing my experiences and to also help build this community in giving everyone the best experience.

The journey begins...
