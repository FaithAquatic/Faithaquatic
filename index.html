About Faith Aquatic

Hello Everyone!

I am Desmond and I have been in this shrimp hobby since 2018. I started with a planted tank initially with a few Fire Red neocaridina shrimps but had little success keeping them. Then, I slowly began my search for answers on several online forums & websites but I still couldn't find any reliable source of information. Along the way, I found some local breeders & reached out to them for help. The exciting journey starts from here and my passion for shrimps grew even deeper!
